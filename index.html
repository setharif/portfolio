Ask Any Questions

What services do you offer?

I provide web development, e-commerce solutions, WordPress development, custom software, and website maintenance.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Yes! I can modernize your old website into a clean, responsive, and user-friendly design.

How long does it take to build a website?

It depends on the project size and requirements. A simple website usually takes 3–4 days, while more complex projects may take longer.

What do I need to provide to start my website project?

You’ll only need to share your content (text, images, and logo) and requirements. I’ll handle everything else.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All my websites are fully responsive and optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.

Do you provide support after the website is completed?

Yes, I offer maintenance and technical support to ensure your website runs smoothly.

Do you offer SEO (Search Engine Optimization) services?

Yes, i set up basic SEO for all websites and also provide advanced SEO services to help your site rank on Google.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept bank transfer, EasyPaisa, JazzCash, and PayPal (for international clients).

Will I get updates during the project?

Definitely! I provide regular updates and you’ll be able to share feedback at every stage.

Can you deliver a website urgently?

Yes, I do offer fast-track delivery for urgent projects (additional charges may apply).
